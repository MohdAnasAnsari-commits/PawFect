Pet Care Tips & Recommendations

How to Pet

  • Approach slowly and let your pet sniff you.
  • Pet gently around the head and back.
  • Always watch for signs of discomfort.

Pet Care Tips

  • Feed a balanced diet suitable for your pet's age.
  • Regular exercise is important for physical and mental health.
  • Schedule routine vet check-ups and vaccinations.

Recommended Products

  • Shampoo: PetSafe Oatmeal Shampoo
  • Food: Royal Canin Puppy/Kitten Formula
  • Toys: Chew toys and interactive balls
